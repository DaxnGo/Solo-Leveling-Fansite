The Story

The story follows Sung Jin-Woo, the weakest Hunter ranked as an E-rank. Despite his lack of strength, he continues working as a Hunter to support his sick mother and younger sister. During a dangerous dungeon raid, Jin-Woo and his party are trapped in a dungeon with a hidden "Double Dungeon."

This new area contains a deadly trial where almost everyone in the group is killed. Jin-Woo sacrifices himself to save his teammates, but instead of dying, he awakens in a hospital with a mysterious ability: a "System" that allows him to level up and grow stronger, like a character in a video game.

Characters

Sung Jin-Woo

The main protagonist who evolves from the weakest E-rank hunter to become an unstoppable force. His unique ability to level up transforms him into one of the most powerful beings in existence.

Cha Hae-In

An S-rank hunter known for her exceptional sword skills and unique ability to smell other hunters. She becomes increasingly interested in Sung Jin-Woo as his powers grow.

Go Gun-hee

Go Gun-hee is the Chairman of the Korean Hunter Association. He plays a pivotal role in supporting Jin-Woo and protecting humanity from powerful threats.

Fun Fact

  • Sung Jin-Woo's powers are based on a unique leveling system.
  • Cha Hae-In's sword skills are unmatched among S-rank hunters.
  • Go Gun-hee's leadership plays a crucial role in the story's progression.

Shadows

Igris

Loyal and disciplined, often likened to a knight. Igris is one of Jin-Woo's strongest and most reliable shadows.

Iron

Aggressive and durable, though somewhat clumsy in personality.

Beru

Fierce and ruthless in battle but comical when seeking Jin-Woo’s approval.

Fun Fact

  • Igris is one of Sung Jin-Woo's most loyal shadow soldiers, often regarded as an honorable knight and a reliable protector in many battles.
  • Iron’s main role in battle is to act as a tank, using his enormous shield to protect others and draw enemy attention. .
  • Beru was originally the fearsome Ant King and one of Sung Jin-Woo's toughest enemies during the Jeju Island Raid. After his defeat, he became one of Jin-Woo's strongest shadow soldiers.

Powers and Abilities

Jin-Woo's unique "Player" ability allows him to grow stronger by completing quests, defeating enemies, and collecting items. Notable powers include:

  • Shadow Army Jin-Woo can summon and control an army of shadows.
  • Stealth Allows him to move undetected by enemies.
  • Infinite Growth His leveling ability has no limits.
